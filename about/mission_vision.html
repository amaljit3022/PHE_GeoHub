Mission & Vision

Mission

The mission is to leverage space-based applications to improve the planning, design, and implementation of water supply and sanitation infrastructure, primarily in rural areas. This includes:

  • Enhancing water supply planning and monitoring using satellite-based systems for JJM Assam.
  • Accurate geotagging of pipelines and infrastructure with GPS-enabled apps and dashboards.
  • Validating water source points through remote sensing and Digital Elevation Models (DEMs).
  • Real-time field monitoring using GIS-enabled mobile applications.
  • Coordinating with BISAG-N and integrating verified GIS layers into PM Gati Shakti portal.
  • Institutionalizing space applications via a dedicated GIS Cell in the Mission Director’s office.

Vision

PHED Assam’s vision is to integrate advanced space technology and build internal capacity to address critical challenges in water resource management, climate resilience, and public health. Goals include:

  • Advanced aquifer and groundwater modeling using satellite data like NISAR.
  • Monitoring river behavior and surface source sustainability via remote sensing.
  • Flood risk mapping and climate adaptation planning using satellite flood models.
  • Mapping solar feasibility and promoting solar-powered systems for energy savings.
  • Assessing water quality risks and pollution source mapping through satellite indices.
  • Integrating IoT sensors with satellite connectivity for real-time monitoring.
  • Establishing command centers and training staff in geospatial technologies.
