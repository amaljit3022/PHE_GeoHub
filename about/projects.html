GIS-Enabled Projects

Completed Projects

  • Hydro-geomorphological Maps (2008) – Identified potential groundwater zones with NRSC/ISRO support.
  • Scheme-wise Mapping – Daily mapping using GPS data and base imagery for DPR preparation.

Ongoing Projects (JJM Assam)

  • GIS-Based Pipeline Mapping – Geotagging of pipeline networks with mobile GPS apps, visualized in QGIS and dashboards.
  • Integration with PM Gati Shakti – Submitting verified scheme GIS layers to BISAG-N for integrated infrastructure planning.
  • Source Validation using Remote Sensing – DEM-based hydrological modeling and analysis for sustainable water sources.
  • Smart Field Monitoring – Real-time geotagging of lithological and asset data using mobile apps.

Future Plans

  • Aquifer & Recharge Mapping – Groundwater zone modeling using NISAR radar and lithology data.
  • Flood Risk Mapping – Identify vulnerable assets and integrate hazard maps in Bhuvan-PHED Dashboard.
  • Water Quality Index Mapping – Use satellite indicators to detect contamination risks (arsenic/iron).
  • IoT + Satcom Integration – Real-time sensor data transmission from remote assets.
  • Solar Feasibility Mapping – Use irradiance data to optimize solar pump deployment.
  • Greywater Management – GIS-based site selection for soak pits and DEWATS.
  • Pollution Source Mapping – Geotagging of industrial/sewage discharge zones integrated with sensor data.
  • Groundwater Resilience Modeling – Catchment zoning, recharge planning, and trend monitoring.
